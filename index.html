Renewable Energy

Renewable energy comes from natural sources that are replenished faster than they are consumed. It includes solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, and biomass energy. Unlike fossil fuels, renewable sources produce little to no greenhouse gas emissions, making them essential in combating climate change. Embracing renewable energy is key to creating a sustainable and cleaner future.
